Services

Welcome to BFF Pet Care Services! My name is Brenda Barton and I'm here to provide exercise and enrichment for your furry best friend, and to put your mind at ease. BFF provides a fun, safe, and secure experience for your dog.
Pets add quality to our lives, they touch our hearts and soothe our souls. We owe it to them to return the favor and provide the best life we can for them. However, sometimes our busy schedules make that difficult. BFF offers daily walks to ensure that your pet gets the fresh air and activity they need to stay happy and healthy. Dogs from individual households are walked in their own neighborhood and receive undivided attention.
Physical exercise and mental engagement are both equally necessary to create balance and help counter undesirable behaviors from excess energy. Every dog has different requirements and motivations. I am mindful about recognizing teachable moments to tailor walks to be interesting and rewarding. Even small challenges encountered along our route can be playfully utilized to overcome fears, practice problem solving skills, or simply improve balance. More than just physical exercise, they will be getting quality time that will make them stronger, braver, better, because I always set them up for success.
My goal is to help your furpal stay fit and be the best family member and polite community citizen that they can be. Your pooch will be content and relaxed when you arrive home! A tired, confident pup is a good pup!
Appropriate precautions are always observed during extreme weather conditions to ensure your pets comfort and safety, and client's preferred training practices are followed. You'll be kept informed of our adventures through a daily text and picture.
So, while you're away at work, attending an event or joining friends for a spontaneous get together, I can step in to walk your pooch or provide a potty break and a scratch behind the ears!

Meet and Greet

I strongly believe that every client deserves a healthy relationship with their pet care provider based on reliability, familiarity, and trust. You are allowing me into your home to take care of a significant member of your family and to me this is a very important responsibility. The Meet and Greet is an opportunity for us to get to know each other, I can answer all of your questions, go over paperwork, and together we can develop a routine that best suits you and your dog. Consults are scheduled Monday to Friday late afternoons, and Monday to Thursday into the early evening, subject to availability. Allow 30-45 minutes for the consult, and please have a copy of your dog’s vaccination records for me, as well as 2 copies of your house key unless a lockbox is to be used. Give us a call and we’ll bring the doggie treats!

About


My name is Brenda Barton, and I have always been a huge animals-of-all-kinds lover and have lived on a farm for the last 25 years. During that time, I have owned and enjoyed caring for all manner of critters including dogs, of course, cats, sheep, llamas, goats, pigs, poultry, and beef cows. I have owned and been enchanted by horses since 1991, having at least two and as many as four at any given time; my husband and I currently live with three on our small farm in Duvall, along with our one dog, three cats, pigs, chickens, ducks and guinea fowl. I believe animals can be an important addition to our quality of life, and it’s important to me to provide a happy, healthy, balanced life for them.
